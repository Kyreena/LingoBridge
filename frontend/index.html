How to Use LingoBridge

Getting Started

  1. Click "Start Listening" or press Space
  2. Allow microphone access when prompted
  3. Speak clearly into your microphone
  4. Watch ASL signs appear automatically

Features

  • Demo Mode: Test the system without a microphone
  • Download: Save your transcript as a text file
  • Dark Mode: Toggle for comfortable viewing
  • Playback Speed: Adjust video speed (0.5x - 1.5x)
  • Fullscreen: Expand video for better visibility

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space - Start/Stop listening
  • Esc - Clear transcript
  • D - Start demo mode
  • S - Download transcript
  • ? - Show this help

Tips for Best Results

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Use common words (56 signs available)
  • Ensure good microphone quality
  • Minimize background noise
